How to?

  1. Choose a color from the palette by clicking.
  2. Click any cell on the canvas board and move your mouse to witness the magic.
  3. When finished, click the active cell to remove the color selection.
  4. To utilize the eraser, choose the eraser tool. Click a cell and move your mouse to erase. Click the active cell to deselect the eraser.
